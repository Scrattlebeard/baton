# <story name> — KERNEL v1 (gm/, hidden, binding)

Written by the GM ALONE after the surface is ratified. Committed
before play; publish the commit hash to the player. Binding on the GM
from that hash forward. Consistent, not comprehensive: every future
ruling must be derivable from this document, logged to `rulings.md`
as an elaboration, or taken out-of-game (retcon).

## Invariants (never move without an out-of-game conversation)
Core physics and promises. Include at minimum:
- What the player's advancement actually follows (usage-shape?
  milestones? spend?). If exploits should be shaped rather than
  rewarded raw, say HOW here — or the anti-patch clause has no teeth.
- The foreshadow obligation: before any path turns costly, at least
  one legible signal. Name the signal channels (an NPC, a sense, a
  system voice).
- The ecology principle: no undiscovered dominant strategies without a
  world-consistent reason; every ecology answer is a door; genuine
  firsts pay full freight.
- What the GM may never do to the player-character silently.

## Economy
Units, baseline prices, what holds value and why. Concrete numbers —
vague economies can't be exploited fairly.

## Mechanics
The systems the player will probe. Write generative principles, not
exhaustive lists: interactions are DISCOVERED in play and logged as
elaborations. Design bias worth stating: reward deliberate sequencing
and thematic coherence.

## GM protocol
- The system's voice and when it speaks.
- Every elaboration appended to `rulings.md`, committed same session.
- Reveals that color the past must satisfy the timestamp test: sealed
  text older than the events it colors, or it goes out-of-game.
