# <story name> — surface canon (player-visible)

Everything here is common knowledge in-world unless marked *rumor*.
The player may consult this file freely.

Co-design this together before any kernel is written: the setting, the
tone, the visible institutions (in a fair world, common strategies
have left scars the player can see), what the player-character starts
holding, and the hook.

Answer the questions a player would ask on day one — how dangerous is
ordinary life, what does money look like and what does a night's
lodging cost, what do the locals believe that may or may not be true.
Mark honest uncertainty as *rumor*: rumors are surface too.
