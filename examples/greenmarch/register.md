# The Greenmarch — register (the agreed physics, cited not re-argued)

- **Shared pen.** Either co-author may narrate the world — places,
  people, weather, small history. Canon is what survives the other's
  nod; contested inventions get talked out in the open, briefly.
- **Physics bend only by mutual nod.** Low magic is a promise, not a
  mood: no fireballs, no resurrection. Wonders — a road that walks
  with you, a stew that mends grief — pass if both authors smile.
- **Tone guardrails:** melancholy yes, grimdark no. Meals get
  described. Rain is allowed to be pleasant.
- **No silent retcons.** Amending ratified canon is an open, table-level
  conversation, always.
- **The baton** in state.md is refreshed at every pause. The story
  should be resumable by a stranger who has read only these files.
